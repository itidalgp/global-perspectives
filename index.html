Monitoring and Documenting Illegal Infrastructure

OUR AGENDA

Itidal.gp is dedicated to the identification and analysis of unauthorized infrastructure developments. Our aim is to spread awareness against illegal infrastructure in our school by doing class discussions etc to make the youth aware of the consequences of unauthorized development and responsible development.
